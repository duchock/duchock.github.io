Artful Things


This is a collection of a number of costumes, fiber art, and other random creative things I've created over the past few years. Pieces are generally displayed in chronological order within their categories.


Needlepoint


#008 v #025
Year: 2020
This was my first needlepoint I made. I picked up needlepoint incidentally as a pandemic hobby, though I had already been considering it after having done my first big piece with the SMRPG map. It was a great learning experience - everything was new territory. DMC wool, mono canvas.
Guy
Year: 2020
This was my second project. A very ambitious jump. This piece was made while I was having thoughts about my father. DMC wool, and it was a huge pain tracking down enough shades of red. Pretty happy with how it turned out.
NYPD
Year: 2021
A still from the Comix Zone intro, from a panned shot stitching together about 2 screens in length. This one is notable for being my mother's favorite. It's also probably the best thing I've ever created in life. I swapped to Waverly Wool and interlocked canvas with this one.
Drugs
Year: 2021
Created as a gift for a good friend. This was my first step into trying to scale up my pieces in size. It was okay.
U dancing mad?
Year: 2022
One of my dream projects. I would have loved to do the complete tower, but good sense convinced me otherwise.

Cross-Stitch


The Treachery of Surrealism
Year: 2008
This was my first cross-stitch I made. Pattern most likely came from Sprite Stitch.
Two Girls, One Up
Year: 2009...ish
I'm not too fond of this one. The joke ended up being way more overdone than I expected it to be. You can tell it was never preserved properly, and that it's been sitting in a closet since it was finished.
Ai love
Year: 2010
I don't really know why I made this, neither being sentimental of the idea of love or simple patterns. It reads "I love you" in Japanese.
Classes
Year: 2017
This was the most ambitious but also the laziest piece I ever worked on. It took roughly a year to finish, but mostly due to a lack of willpower than anything else.
SMRPG map
Year: 2018
Largest piece I've ever made. I've played through this game several times - and it's just involved enough to be impressive, but not too large to be impossible to finish.
Catchphrase
Year: 2019
A gift for my mother. This is a collection of sayings from when I was a kid.
PSI
Year: 2020
I came up with this pattern by trying my hand at one of those helvetica ampersand shirt designs, but thought it looked too bland, so I added sprites. This is also my first attempt at blackwork.
